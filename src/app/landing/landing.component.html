What does the test do?

This test is about your personality! We split the personality into two catergories: introvert and extrovert. The test will ask some statements about your personal behaviour and actions. After answering these statements, the result will be calculated and shown on the next page. Based on these results and the answers you have given during the test, we will give you some tips about the type of person you are and how to furture boost your confidence.

How is the result calculated?

To give an accurate result about the answered statements an AI is used. This AI is trained with the answers of about 7000+ people. The only thing it will output is if you are an introvert or extrovert type of person. The results of the test gave more introverts then extrovert types of person so the AI is more bias to determine you as an introvert person.

What will happen with my information?

The answers you give on the test are saved to our servers to analyze later. If you are not comfortable with this you can turn it off at the end of the test. We also ask a small bit of information about yourself to improve our AI in the future.
